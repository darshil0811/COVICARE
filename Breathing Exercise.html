Spirometer

A spirometer measures the amount of air you can breathe out in one second and the total volume of air you can exhale in one forced breath. These measurements will be compared with a normal result for someone of your age, height and sex, which will help show if your lungs aren't working properly. Your result is considered normal if your score is 80 percent or more of the predicted value.
